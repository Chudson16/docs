matcher-type
````````````

**Options:** *flann |  bow*

Matcher algorithm, Fast Library for Approximate Nearest Neighbors or Bag of Words. FLANN is slower, but more stable. BOW is faster, but can sometimes miss valid matches. . Default: ``flann``
